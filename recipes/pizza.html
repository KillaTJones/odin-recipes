Pizza Recipe

Back to Home

Description

This homemade pizza recipe is perfect for parties! Everyone loves pizza, and your guests can customize their toppings to suit their personal tastes.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup of pizza sauce
  • 8 oz. of slicced mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
  • 10 fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500
  2. Sprerad the pizza sauce evenly on the pizza dough
  3. Top with mozzarella and tomatoes
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes
  5. Remove from oven and top with Basil
  6. Enjoy!
